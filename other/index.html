I know you are nervous. I was too. I got there late about two hours, and all my friends were cleaning the kitchen and I was eating. They took my stuff to the cabin, and we went to play a game. I saw all the campers and got excited. Some of them I knew but some I didn't. My best friend saw me and ran to me and gave me the biggest hug ever. When you go to camp, you know that feeling. That feeling of home is there. It makes you the happiest person in the world.
